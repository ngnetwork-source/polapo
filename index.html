The Official World Polapo Ranking, which is endorsed by Nico Gibbs and the five professional players which make up the Official Assosiation of Proffesional Polapo (OAPP), is issued every day, following completion of the previous days's scores from around the world. This statistic is the number of points earned in total in the players lifetime of Polapo. These points are awarded based upon scoring points on the field. The points are marked down and recorded.
